Due:

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin Case
    • DBx1 sewing needles 90/14
    • Large Flat Head Screwdriver
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in desired color(s)
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Clear ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Frixion brand pen(s)
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Paper Scissors
    • Transfer Paper
    • Serrated Transfer Wheel
    • Seam Ripper
    • Tape Measure
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Pressing and understitching.
  • Installing the button and buttonhole.
  • Installing the shoulder pads.
  • Completing the hem.
  • Sewing thread tacks to the facing.

After Class:

  • Bring final garment and prepare slide deck presentation for next class meeting.
